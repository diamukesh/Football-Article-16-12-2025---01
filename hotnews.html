Mohamed Salah Clash

Mohamed Salah Timeline

Early October 2025

Salah is occasionally benched in some games as Liverpool tries different tactical setups and his form dips. Fans notice he’s not always starting.

Late November 2025

Salah is left out of the starting XI for multiple Premier League matches in a row, including games against West Ham and Leeds. This is unusual given his status at the club.

Dec 6, 2025

He is an unused substitute in Liverpool’s 3-3 draw with Leeds. After the match, Salah gives a pointed interview saying he feels the club has “thrown me under the bus” and that his relationship with manager Arne Slot has broken down.

Dec 8, 2025

Salah is left out of the squad for Liverpool’s Champions League trip to Inter Milan amid the fallout from his comments.

Turkey Football Betting Scandal

Turkey Timeline

27 October 2025

TFF audit reveals 371 referees had betting accounts, 152 were actively betting, violating FIFA and TFF rules.

31 October 2025

Istanbul Chief Public Prosecutor’s Office launches criminal investigation into referees’ betting activities.

10–11 November 2025

1,024 players suspended from professional football for alleged betting violations; lower division matches postponed.

11 November 2025

Eight arrests, including a top club chairman and referees; TFF calls it a “moral crisis” in Turkish football.

13 November 2025

102 professional players banned by TFF’s disciplinary board across Süper Lig and second division.

18 November 2025

281 players sanctioned; bans range from short-term to long-term exclusions from football.

26 November 2025

More than 1,100 suspensions across referees and players; lists published for transparency.

05 December 2025

Additional arrests, 46 individuals detained including club presidents, players, and referees.
